Outreach

Our goal is to provide as many K-16 students as possible with opportunities to be exposed to STEM discovery and research opportunities and to motivate them to pursue STEM education. We coach and train our students to become outstanding technical leaders and role models in our communities.

  • Outreach with Marist High School Robotics Team
  • Keynote at FIRST Regional Championships with Nicolet High School FIRST Team members
  • High School Outreach at University of Virgin Islands
  • Montessori School HEIR Lab Visit
  • Outreach in San Juan, Puerto Rico at University of Turabo
